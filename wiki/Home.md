## About
NLZiet Kodi Addon is an unofficial addon for Kodi that brings NLZiet live TV, series, and movies directly to your Kodi device. It is under active development and some features may be incomplete or broken. Please see the Known Issues & FAQ for more details.

For the latest release and installation instructions, visit the [GitHub Releases page](https://github.com/Nigel1992/NLZiet-Kodi-Addon/releases).
