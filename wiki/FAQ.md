# FAQ & Known Issues

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Search is not working, is this a bug?**
A: Search is currently a placeholder and will be implemented in a future update.

**Q: Why are there no categories for movies or series?**
A: Categories will be added in a future release.

**Q: Where is the full EPG guide?**
A: A full EPG guide is planned but not yet available.

**Q: The add-on fails to play some streams.**
A: Ensure you have `inputstream.adaptive` enabled and your NLZiet subscription is active.

**Q: Is this add-on official?**
A: No, this is an unofficial community project and not affiliated with NLZiet.
